Project Breakdown

The corporation was founded by my friend, Sipesihle Sibeko, with a bold vision: to create gamified educational experiences tailored specifically for neurodivergent learners. Recognizing the untapped potential of learners with ADHD, autism, dyslexia, dyspraxia, and other conditions, the corporation is driven by a mission to make education both inclusive and engaging. By focusing on individual strengths and leveraging Howard Gardner’s Multiple Intelligences Theory, we develop games that not only teach but empower. Whether it’s using rhythm to teach math or interactive puzzles to foster problem-solving, the goal is to create meaningful, strength-based learning experiences.

As part of the team, I play a key role in shaping the direction and design of our games. My responsibilities include conceptualizing gameplay mechanics that align with each intelligence category, designing intuitive user interfaces, and ensuring our games are accessible to diverse players. I also collaborate on research efforts to gather insights from neurodivergent individuals, ensuring our designs are informed by real experiences. Working alongside Sipesihle and the rest of the team, I’m proud to contribute my skills in game development and my passion for inclusivity to a project that aims to redefine what education can look like for all minds. Together, we are building a platform where neurodivergent learners can thrive, one game at a time.
